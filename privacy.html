What data do we collect?

BeKind collects the following data:
  • Personal identification information (Name, Email, Picture, Bio)
  • Login information (Password, Google authentication, Facebook authentication)
  • Location information (State, Country, Geolocation)
  • Acts of Kindness information (Story, Pictures, Geolocation, Comments, Likes)
  • Chat information (Peer-To-Peer messages, Group messages)
  • Contact information (Address Book, Facebook contacts)
How do we collect your data?

You directly provide BeKind with most of the data we collect. We collect data and process data when you:
  • Sign up or login.
  • Edit your profile.
  • Submit or interact with an act of kindness.
  • Send chat messages.
  • Invite friends.
How will we use your data?

BeKind collects your data so that we can:
  • Manage your account and acts of kindness.
  • Add people to your friends list and groups.
  • Display public user information and acts.
  • Display users and acts by location.
  • Enable communication between users.
How do we store your data?

BeKind securely stores your data on your phone and on Firebase from Google LLC. Information on Firebase privacy and security can be found in https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy. BeKind will keep your data on Firebase until contacted to remove, or until data removal is recommended by system administration for the efficient functioning of the app.

What are your data protection rights?

BeKind would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:
  • The right to access – You have the right to request BeKind for copies of your personal data. We may charge you a small fee for this service.
  • The right to rectification – You have the right to request that BeKind correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request BeKind to complete the information you believe is incomplete.
  • The right to erasure – You have the right to request that BeKind erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to restrict processing – You have the right to request that BeKind restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to object to processing – You have the right to object to BeKind’s processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to data portability – You have the right to request that BeKind transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.
If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us.

Privacy policies of other apps and websites

The BeKind website contains links to other apps and websites. Our privacy policy applies only to our website, so if you click on a link to another app or website, you should read their privacy policy.

Changes to our privacy policy

BeKind keeps its privacy policy under regular review and places any updates on this page.

Contact us

If you have any questions about BeKind’s privacy policy, the data we hold on you, or you would like to exercise one of your data protection rights, please do not hesitate to contact us.
